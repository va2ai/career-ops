## 5. What "Forward Deployed Engineer" Means at Bland

A **customer-embedded full-stack/AI engineer**, not an SE or CSM. Day-to-day: discovery → design → build → deploy → expand for an enterprise account portfolio; build tailored voice agents (pathways, prompts, API integrations into CRMs/ticketing/data); iterate from production call traces; on-site at HQ and customer sites as the technical face of Bland.

Customers cluster in **regulated, high-call-volume verticals**: Better.com (mortgage/fintech), Kin Insurance, Signant Health (clinical trials), Sears, Samsara, TripAdvisor, Snapchat, Gallup, Drata, Copart. SOC-II + HIPAA + GDPR posture is load-bearing.

Required: REST/JSON, Python or JS, LLM/AI SDK integration, full-stack ownership, 0–10 yrs, strong comms. Nice-to-haves: founder/startup background, customer discovery, creative portfolio.
